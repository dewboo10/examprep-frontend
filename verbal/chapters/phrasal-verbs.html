Common Phrasal Verbs

A comprehensive collection of 100 commonly used phrasal verbs with their meanings and examples. Perfect for CAT, XAT, and government exam preparation.

1. Break down
To stop working or functioning
Example: My car broke down on the way to work.
2. Call off
To cancel something
Example: They called off the meeting due to bad weather.
3. Carry out
To perform or complete a task
Example: The scientists carried out several experiments.
4. Come across
To find or discover something by chance
Example: I came across an old photo album in the attic.
5. Cut down
To reduce the amount of something
Example: I'm trying to cut down on sugar.
6. Deal with
To handle or manage a situation
Example: She knows how to deal with difficult customers.
7. Fall apart

8. Get along
