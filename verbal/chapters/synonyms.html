Emotions & Feelings

Happy

Synonyms: Joyful, content, delighted, pleased, cheerful, elated, jubilant, blissful, satisfied, ecstatic
Example: She was happy to see her old friend after many years.
Sad

Synonyms: Unhappy, sorrowful, melancholy, miserable, despondent, dejected, downcast, gloomy, forlorn, woeful
Example: The sad news brought tears to everyone's eyes.
Angry
Feeling or showing strong displeasure or hostility
Synonyms: Furious, enraged, livid, irate, mad, infuriated, outraged, wrathful, irritated, fuming
Example: He was angry about the unfair treatment.
Calm
Free from agitation or strong emotions
Synonyms: Peaceful, composed, serene, tranquil, placid, relaxed, unruffled, collected, cool-headed
Example: She remained calm during the crisis.
Excited
Feeling or showing enthusiasm and eagerness
Synonyms: Thrilled, enthusiastic, eager, animated, elated, exhilarated, energized, passionate, spirited, zealous
Example: She was excited about her upcoming trip.
Anxious
Feeling worried, nervous, or uneasy
Synonyms: Worried, nervous, tense, uneasy, concerned, apprehensive, troubled, agitated, restless, fretful
Example: He felt anxious before the important presentation.
Confident
Feeling or showing certainty about something
Synonyms: Self-assured, certain, positive, sure, convinced, assertive, bold, self-confident, poised, composed
Example: She was confident about her ability to succeed.
Joyful
Feeling, expressing, or causing great pleasure and happiness
Synonyms: Happy, delighted, cheerful, merry, glad, elated, ecstatic, jubilant, overjoyed, thrilled
Example: The joyful celebration lasted all night.
Melancholy
A feeling of pensive sadness, typically with no obvious cause
Synonyms: Sad, gloomy, depressed, dejected, downcast, despondent, blue, mournful, somber, wistful
Example: The melancholy music reflected his mood.
Furious
Extremely angry
Synonyms: Angry, enraged, infuriated, irate, livid, incensed, wrathful, outraged, fuming, seething
Example: He was furious about the unfair treatment.
Ecstatic
Feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness or joyful excitement
Synonyms: Elated, euphoric, overjoyed, thrilled, delighted, jubilant, exultant, rapturous, blissful, transported
Example: She was ecstatic about winning the competition.
Despondent

Synonyms: Disheartened, discouraged, dejected, downcast, depressed, gloomy, melancholy, forlorn, crestfallen, dispirited
Example: The despondent team after their loss.
Indignant
Feeling or showing anger or annoyance at what is perceived as unfair treatment
Synonyms: Angry, resentful, outraged, irate, furious, incensed, wrathful, enraged, offended, aggrieved
Example: She was indignant at the unfair accusation.
