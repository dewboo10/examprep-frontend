Critical Reasoning

Master the art of critical reasoning with our comprehensive collection of practice questions. Learn to analyze arguments, identify assumptions, and evaluate conclusions.

All Strengthen Weaken Assumption Conclusion

Argument:

Government spending on healthcare should be increased to improve the country's overall well-being.

Reasoning:

The healthcare system is underfunded and unable to provide basic services to all citizens.

Which of the following statements best supports the argument?

A) People have to rely on private hospitals due to a lack of public healthcare facilities.
B) Many countries with better healthcare systems have seen a decrease in the national mortality rate.
C) There is a growing demand for high-end private healthcare services in urban areas.
D) Healthcare costs have skyrocketed due to advances in medical technology.

Correct Answer: B) Many countries with better healthcare systems have seen a decrease in the national mortality rate.

This option provides strong support for the argument by showing a direct correlation between better healthcare systems and improved national well-being through reduced mortality rates.

Statement:

The government should invest more in renewable energy sources to mitigate the impacts of climate change.

Reasoning:

Renewable energy reduces carbon emissions, which contribute to global warming.

Which of the following would weaken the argument above?

A) The cost of renewable energy technologies has significantly reduced in recent years.
B) Fossil fuels are still more cost-effective and efficient for many industries.
C) Many countries have successfully adopted renewable energy and reduced their carbon emissions.
D) Renewable energy sources are highly dependent on weather conditions and are not always reliable.

Correct Answer: B) Fossil fuels are still more cost-effective and efficient for many industries.

This option weakens the argument by suggesting that the economic and practical benefits of fossil fuels might outweigh the environmental benefits of renewable energy.

Premise:

Conclusion:

Which of the following is an assumption made in the argument?

A) Sleeping for more than eight hours improves focus and concentration during exams.
B) Students who sleep less than eight hours a day perform poorly in exams.
C) The study was conducted on students from diverse educational backgrounds.
D) Academic performance is the only factor influenced by the amount of sleep students get.

Correct Answer: B) Students who sleep less than eight hours a day perform poorly in exams.

This is the key assumption in the argument. The conclusion assumes that less sleep leads to poor performance, which is necessary to justify the recommendation.
