Arrange the following sentences in the correct order to form a coherent paragraph:
A. The advent of the Internet has revolutionized the way businesses operate.
B. Online platforms now facilitate easier communication, product promotion, and customer engagement.
C. Over time, these platforms have enabled companies to reach global audiences and expand their reach.
D. In the last decade, the role of e-commerce has increased, contributing immensely to the digital economy.
E. As a result, businesses are increasingly relying on digital strategies to stay competitive in the market.

Correct Order: A, B, D, C, E

The paragraph starts with the introduction of the Internet's impact (A), followed by its effects on business operations (B). Then it discusses the growth of e-commerce (D), its global reach (C), and concludes with the current business reliance on digital strategies (E).
