My Mission

To break down the barriers that make education a luxury. I believe every student deserves access to quality exam preparation resources, regardless of their financial background.

My Vision

To create a world where quality education is accessible to everyone. I envision a future where no student is left behind due to financial constraints.

My Story

Hi, I'm a passionate developer and lifelong learner who built ParikshaPrep from scratch. This project was born from my frustration with seeing how education, which should be a fundamental right, has become an expensive luxury that many students cannot afford.

As someone who has experienced the challenges of competitive exam preparation, I understand the financial burden that quality education resources can place on students and their families. I created this platform because I believe that every student, regardless of their economic background, deserves access to the best preparation materials.

This is not just a website - it's my personal mission to democratize education. I've poured my heart into building a comprehensive, user-friendly platform that provides everything a student needs to succeed, completely free of cost. My goal is simple: to make quality education accessible to everyone who dreams of achieving their potential.
